About Cognara Training and Auctoritas

Auctoritas is built by Cognara Training.

Cognara Training is an AI-focused corporate training provider in Australia, building better training and safety solutions for medium business. Auctoritas extends that mission into workplace knowledge, compliance, and on-demand support.

Why Auctoritas exists

From training design to practical workplace support

Cognara Training’s public positioning focuses on training design and implementation, safety training, AI training, and workplace clarity. Auctoritas can be presented as the product expression of that same mission: helping organizations make knowledge more usable, training more practical, and compliance easier to navigate.

  • ✓AI-focused training perspective
    Use AI to make knowledge more useful, not just more automated.
  • ✓Application-first design
    Make sure people can apply what they were taught when they need it.
  • ✓Clarity and transparency
    Help teams navigate workplace rules and training content with more confidence.

Company context

Cognara Training’s public profile highlights corporate training, training design and implementation, safety training, AI training, and corporate law training for non-lawyers. Auctoritas extends those themes into a product experience.
